Before You Book
Please read the guidelines carefully
  • 01
    Valid ID Required
    Carry a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar / Passport / DL) at pickup. No ID = no keys.
  • 02
    Driver's Licence
    A valid driving licence for the vehicle type is mandatory. Unlicensed bookings are cancelled without refund.
  • 03
    Timely Pick-Up & Return
    Arrive within 15 minutes of start time. Late returns billed at ₹50 per 30 min.
  • 04
    Fuel & Condition
    Return with the same fuel level and condition. Damage or missing fuel is charged separately.
  • 05
    Cancellation Policy
    Free cancellation 2 hrs before start. Within 2 hrs = 50% charge. No-shows billed in full.
  • 06
    Carry Physical Documents to the Shop
    Even though you've uploaded your DL and Aadhaar, you must bring the original physical copies to the shop at pickup. Digital uploads alone are not sufficient — no originals = no vehicle handover.
⚠️ Traffic violations, accidents, or misuse are your sole responsibility during the rental period.
